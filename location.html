Best Sailboat Location

Top

Starting from Mariner's Bay Lighthouse: Head West on Lighthouse Road Start your journey by heading west on Lighthouse Road, keeping the scenic coastline to your left. Continue for about 2 miles until you reach the intersection with Oceanview Boulevard. Turn Left onto Oceanview Boulevard At the intersection, take a left onto Oceanview Boulevard. You’ll pass by several charming seaside cafes and art galleries. Continue straight for 3 miles until you see signs for Port Harbor. Take a Right onto Harbor Drive As you approach the bustling Port Harbor, take a right onto Harbor Drive. You’ll notice the smell of fresh sea air and the sound of seagulls as you drive past the marina. Continue along Harbor Drive for 1 mile. Turn Left onto Seawind Lane After passing the historic Shipwright’s Inn on your right, take a left onto Seawind Lane. This quiet street is lined with palm trees and offers a clear view of the bay. Drive down Seawind Lane for half a mile. Arrive at Captain’s Cove Sailboats & Supplies Look for a large wooden sign with a carved sailboat logo on your right. The shop has a bright blue facade with nautical flags hanging out front. There’s plenty of parking available in the lot beside the shop.
